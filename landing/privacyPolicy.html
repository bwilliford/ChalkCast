Privacy Policy

ChalkCast, Inc. (“ChalkCast, “us”, or “we”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to chalkcast.org, learn.chalkcast.org, our mobile application, and online services (collectively, our “Service”), which are all owned and operated by ChalkCast.

This Privacy Policy describes how ChalkCast collects and uses the information you provide on our Service, and describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information. "You" or “your” means a visitor or a user (whether logged in or not) of our Service.

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and is subject to our Terms of Services, which governs your use of ChalkCast Services.

ChalkCast Privacy Policy Principles

  1. We promise to create a safe and secure environment for students and teachers of all ages.
  2. We do not require you to provide any Personal Information in order to join a class session, which is accessible without a user account.
  3. Privacy drives product design, and we consciously avoid personal information unless it improves the product experience for you.
  4. When we do collect data, we are transparent on what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.
  5. We do not sell any information, personal or otherwise, to third parties.
  6. We do not display advertising or promotional material of any kind on ChalkCast.

On occasion, we collect student and teacher testimonials to promote our learning philosophy. Testimonials are published with the permission of the participating Student, their parent or legal guardian, or Teacher. Testimonials include Personal Information such as name, school, likeness and photo or video of the person being profiled.

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

No Commercial Use of Student Data

We do not sell ads or Personal Information, or use Personal Information for financial gain. Per the Student Online Personal Information Protection Act (SOPIPA), ChalkCast does not use, disclose, or compile any student Personal Information for the purpose of marketing or advertising commercial products or services. We do not disclose any student Personal Information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Technical Data

To provide the best user experience, we may use various technologies to remember your past activity and provide you with a more streamlined process.

For example, whether or not you have a ChalkCast account, when you use ChalkCast for the first time, we send a token that is stored in your browser’s local storage, so that we can tie all future ChalkCast sessions back to you.

Updates

As we add more features to our services, our Privacy Policy will change. However, we will not change how we use any Personal Information we have already collected in any material way without also providing notice of the change and obtaining user consent, or the consent from a school or district, if applicable.

As soon as we implement changes, we will notify users about updates. Within 24 hours of an update, we will post the revised Privacy Policy to our website, and display a notification banner on our homepage, on our sign up page, and any other place on our site we deem to have high traffic. For any significant changes to our policy, we will also contact users directly via email if their email address has been explicitly shared with ChalkCast.

In addition, ChalkCast will maintain a changelog that will document a list of notable changes by date to make it easier for users to track when and what changes were made.
