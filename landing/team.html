Who We Are

We're a team of engineers, designers, strategists, students, and teachers dedicated to making learning more accessible around the world.

Our Mission

We believe that all students, no matter their circumstances, should have resources to reach their highest potential. Our technology delivers a real-time and interactive experience between students and educators, no matter the device or geographic location.

Our Values

Inclusivity

We strive to create an environment where all people feel welcomed, valued, and supported.

Candor

We like real, honest conversations that lead to smart ideas, fast actions and less meetings.

Responsibility

We commit and hold ourselves accountable to each other as teammates, and as members of the education community at large.

Creativity

We give each other the time and space to experiment and take risks.
